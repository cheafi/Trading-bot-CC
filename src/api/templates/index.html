🛡️ Rejection Discipline
These stocks had signals but were rejected by the decision engine. Understanding why not is what separates disciplined systems from bullish machines.
📋 Common Rejection Reasons
🕐 Timing — Signal exists but timing is wrong (extended, overbought, post-earnings)
💧 Liquidity — Volume too thin for reliable execution
📅 Earnings — Binary event too close, risk undefined
👥 Crowded — Too much consensus, risk of snap reversal
📉 Laggard — Sector/stock RS is weak, fighting the trend
🏭 Sector Mismatch — Regime doesn't favor this sector right now
⚡ High Conflict — Bullish and bearish signals cancel each other
📊 Data Stale — Not enough recent data to trust the signal
