CC · Clarity Console — Dashboard Guide
How to use your
trading dashboard
This is a regime-aware signal dashboard — it shows you when to trade, what to trade, and how much to risk. Every element on screen is connected. Follow this guide left-to-right before your first session.
🔝 Step 1 — Read the Header Bar First
The thin coloured strip below the CC logo is the Trust Strip. Before looking at any signal, check these 4 elements from left to right:
① Mode Badge
LIVE PAPER SYNTHETIC
⚠ If you see SYNTHETIC — market data failed to load. Do not act on any signal until this clears to LIVE.
② Regime
BULL SIDEWAYS CHOPPY BEAR
This is the master gate. In BEAR or CHOPPY regime, most signals are auto-rejected even if they look good.
③ Tradeability
STRONG_TRADE TRADE SELECTIVE NO_TRADE
This is your position sizing instruction for the day. SELECTIVE = cut size by 50%. NO_TRADE = no new entries at all.
④ Confidence Bar
Green > 70% = high confidence in regime read. Amber 40–70% = uncertain. Red < 40% = data quality issue — be extra cautious.
🗓 Step 2 — Your Daily Workflow (in order)
1
📋 Brief tab — pre-market read BEFORE OPEN
Open 📋 Brief first. You'll see:
• Macro Summary — overnight news, Fed, earnings calendar
• Regime Assessment — today's market posture in plain English
• Top 3 Setups — highest-ranked tickers the engine flagged
• Sector Rotation — which sectors have institutional money flowing in
The brief is generated pre-market and cached. It does not change during the trading session.
2
🎯 Overview tab — regime + market pulse AT OPEN
The Overview tab shows the live market condition. Key elements to read:
• VIX reading — if it jumped overnight, check tradeability before anything else
• Breadth % — "X% of stocks above 50-day MA" — below 40% = internal weakness
• Signal Funnel (Universe → Triggered → ≥6 → ≥7 → ≥8) — shows how many setups survived each filter
• Sector Leaders / Laggards — teal bars = leading sectors, red = lagging
• Tradeability Instruction — the coloured banner at the top of Overview tells you exactly how to size
3
📊 Ranked tab — find your candidates
Open 📊 Ranked. Each row is a signal card showing:
• Ticker + Action badge — BUY / BUY_ON_DIP / WATCH
• Score bar — green = 8+, amber = 6-7, the longer the bar the stronger the setup
• Conviction tier pill — TRADE LEADER WATCH
• Entry / Stop / Target — shown on the card directly
• R:R badge — must be ≥ 3:1 for TRADE, ≥ 2:1 for WATCH

⚡ Action rule: Only take TRADE conviction signals. Ignore WATCH unless you're building a watchlist.
4
🔍 Dossier — confirm before entry
Click any ticker card to open its Dossier. This is where you confirm the trade. Check in order:
Entry ZoneThe price range the engine expects as a clean entry. Wider zone = buy-on-dip setup. Hard StopYour non-negotiable exit. The engine places this below structural support. Never widen it. Target 1 / 2Price at 2R and 3R. Sell partial at T1, trail stop to breakeven, ride to T2. Factor ChipsGreen chips = what's working for this trade. Red chips = what's against it. The more green the better. Sector PeersAre sector peers also showing strength? Confirmation = safer entry. Why NowThe AI thesis in plain English. Read this — if it doesn't match your own read, skip the trade.
5
💼 Portfolio tab — check capacity before entering
Before placing any order, open 💼 Portfolio and check:
• Open Positions counter — hard cap is 10 positions. If at 10, don't add a new one.
• Sector Exposure bars — no single sector should exceed 30% of portfolio.
• Correlation matrix — if the new ticker correlates > 0.7 with an existing position, skip it (same risk exposure).
• Heat gauge — if portfolio heat is in the red zone, reduce size or wait.
6
🚫 Rejections tab — learn why signals fail
Open 🚫 Rejections to see every signal the engine blocked and exactly why. Common rejection reasons:
VIX > 35Crisis circuit breaker fired — no entries allowed regardless of score.
REGIME=BEARRegime gate blocked the signal. Wait for regime to shift.
RS < 90Stock underperforming SPY — no entry until RS recovers.
R:R < 2:1Setup doesn't meet minimum risk/reward. Reward too small or stop too wide.
SCORE < 6Not enough signal factors aligned. Missing trend / volume / structure confirmation.

NVDA 8.7 Score 8.7/10 — strong setup. The green border = TRADE conviction. Scores below 7 get an amber border (WATCH), below 6 don't appear here.
STOP $127.20 is your hard stop. Risk = $132.40 − $127.20 = $5.20 per share = 1R. If price closes below this, exit. No exceptions.
R:R 3:1 Target $148 is 3× the stop distance above entry. This is the minimum threshold for a TRADE signal. Below 3:1, the engine blocks it.
RS LEADER CONFIRMED_LEADER = this stock has been outperforming SPY for 3+ months. This is required for a TRADE conviction signal.
📈 Step 4 — Using the RS Tab
The 📈 RS tab ranks all tracked stocks by their performance vs SPY. Use it to confirm you're only trading the strongest names in the market — not just the ones that look good on a chart.
① Filter by Sector or Cap first
The pill buttons at the top let you narrow to Technology / Healthcare / etc. and MEGA / LARGE / MID / SMALL cap. Always filter to your sector before looking at individual RS scores. The sector filter buttons may load slowly on first click — wait 2s.
② Sector RS Overview (top card)
Shows which sectors are in leadership (RS composite > 110) vs lagging (< 95). The RS 115 green badge = outperforming. The ▲/▼ arrow = direction this week. Trade in the direction of sector strength.
③ RS Breakouts box (left)
These tickers just crossed into RS leadership this week. These are the highest-probability setups in the market right now. Click any ticker to open its Dossier instantly.
④ RS Leaderboard (main table)
Sorted by composite RS score. The columns: RS (composite score), 1W/1M/3M (period returns vs SPY), Δ (week-over-week change), Status (CONFIRMED_LEADER / EMERGING / FADING / BROKEN), Trend (up/down/sideways).

Only trade CONFIRMED_LEADER or EMERGING. Never enter a FADING or BROKEN stock.
🔬 Step 5 — Scanners & Command Centre
🔬 Scanners tab
Click a category pill (Leaders / Breakouts / Pullbacks / Flow) to run that scanner.

• Leaders = stocks with RS > 110 + breakout-ready base
• Breakouts = VCP / tight-base pivot breakouts with volume
• Pullbacks = leaders that pulled back to support — best risk/reward
• Flow = unusual options activity, unusual volume

⚠ In NO_TRADE regime all scanners return 0 hits — this is correct behaviour.
🖥 Command tab
The Command Centre is a terminal-style deep-dive view. Use it when you want:

• Full decision output for a specific ticker — all factors, weights, contra-signals
• Agent journal — history of AI agent runs with outcome tracking
• Reliability stats — how accurate past TRADE calls have been
• Shadow predictions — forward price predictions with unresolved status

This tab is for power users. Most users only need Ranked + Dossier.
