📘 Strategy Guide

📈 Swing Trading
2-10 day holds. Buys RSI < 35 crossing above 20-SMA while above 50-SMA. Best in neutral/low-vol.
🚀 Breakout / VCP
Volume-confirmed breakouts above 20-day highs. Requires 1.5x volume. Best in risk-on uptrends.
⚡ Momentum
Trend-following with 20/50 SMA alignment + RSI 50-75. Best in risk-on markets.
🔁 Mean Reversion
Buys oversold (RSI < 30, 3%+ below 20-SMA). Tight stops 4%, targets 6%. Best in sideways.
